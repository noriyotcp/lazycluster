1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how lazycluster ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information through our Chrome extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

This Privacy Policy describes how lazycluster collects, uses, and discloses your information through its Chrome extension. We use LocalStorage to store certain preferences, such as theme settings, to enhance your experience. This data is saved locally within your browser and is not transmitted externally.

Specifically, the extension saves the following information to your browser's LocalStorage:

  • Theme settings

This data is processed solely for the functionality of the extension and is not transmitted externally. Data stored in LocalStorage persists until the extension is uninstalled.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information processed by the extension is used to provide and improve its functionality. LocalStorage is used to store your theme preferences, ensuring a consistent experience across sessions.

4. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal information, we do not have any data to secure. All processing is done locally on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

The extension does not share any user-provided personal information with third parties without explicit user consent. If third-party sharing becomes necessary in the future, we will present the information to be shared and the purpose, and obtain your consent before proceeding.
